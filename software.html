Software Engineering

Software engineering is the systematic application of engineering principles to software development. It encompasses requirements analysis, design, coding, testing, and maintenance to create reliable and efficient software systems. Collaboration among developers, project managers, and stakeholders is crucial for delivering high-quality software that meets user needs and business objectives.
